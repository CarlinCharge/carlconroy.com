About Me

I'm a full stack web developer with a love of art and design.

My interest in computers and technology began at an early age. At age 5, I was already hogging the family computer playing games on Windows 95. At age 9, I picked up the violin and fell in love with music. Of course at age 13, I got my hands on the guitar and never looked back.

These days, I love all forms of artistic expression and there hasn't been a more exciting time to be in tech. Programming to me is just that: an art form. I couldn't think of a better way for me to marry my love of arts and technology.

When I'm not programming, you can find me with at the nearest concert venue taking in live music or researching the latest tech drooling over all the pixels the latest screens can push.
